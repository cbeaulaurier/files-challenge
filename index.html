Ziva David

Ziva David is a former character on the popular television show, NCIS. She is played by Cote de Pablo. She was first introduced in the third season of the show, replacing Kate who was killed in action. Before joining the NCIS team, Ziva was an effective and ruthless agent of Mossad, the Israeli national intelligence agency. Her father, Eli David is the leader of Mossad. She left the show in Season 11 in a couple of sad farewell episodes.
